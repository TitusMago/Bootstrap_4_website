About MOSCI

A brief background of MOSCI Engineering.

MOSCI Engineering Private Limited Company is a company that is registered under the rules and regulations of Zimbabwe. The various projects that have been done so far gives us confidence in the way we do our things. Our objective is to operate in the whole of Southern Africa by 2025 and ensure that we deliver the best service ever. By then, our economic objective is to recruit and train more people into the construction and energy industry and become the best company when it comes to technical expertise. Mission

Building sound businesses, relationships and strong communities, caring for each other and the environment so that we provide personal, social, professional and spiritual fulfilment.

Vision

To be the most efficient construction company there is, providing timely service delivery, well within scope and budget.

We value honesty, integrity in delivery and pride in the work we do striving to create long standing bonds with all our clients.
